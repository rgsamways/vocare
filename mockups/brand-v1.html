Vocare — Brand Concepts

Four directions, all leaning on typography rather than an invented symbol — elegant, editorial, closer to "considered professional coaching" than "startup app." Scratch work for reaction, not a decided design.

Soft italic serif — reads warm and human rather than corporate. The slight handwritten quality nods to "care" sitting inside "vocare." Closest to a signature than a logotype.
